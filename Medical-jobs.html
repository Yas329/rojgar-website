About Government Medical Jobs

Government medical jobs are among the most respected careers in India. Every year hospitals, medical colleges and health departments recruit doctors, nurses, pharmacists, technicians and healthcare staff.

Medical jobs provide excellent salary, government facilities, job security and an opportunity to serve society.

Medical Job Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • MBBS required for doctor posts
  • B.Sc Nursing / GNM for nurse posts
  • D.Pharm / B.Pharm for pharmacist jobs
  • Diploma or degree required for technician posts

Age Limit

  • 18 to 40 years for most recruitments
  • Age relaxation available for reserved categories

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which is the best government medical job?

AIIMS Doctor and Specialist posts are considered among the best medical jobs.

Can nurses apply for government medical jobs?

Yes, B.Sc Nursing and GNM qualified candidates can apply for staff nurse jobs.

Are government medical jobs permanent?

Yes, most government medical jobs are permanent and provide pension benefits.

What is the salary of a government Staff Nurse?

Government Staff Nurses generally earn between ₹40,000 and ₹70,000 per month.
