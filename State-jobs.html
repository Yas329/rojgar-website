About State Government Jobs

State Government Jobs are recruitment opportunities released by individual state governments in India. These jobs include administrative, police, teaching, clerical, engineering and revenue department posts.

State jobs provide government job security, stable income, pension benefits and opportunities to work near home state.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which is the best state government job?

State PSC officer posts like SDM and DSP are considered among the best state jobs.

Can 12th pass students apply for state jobs?

Yes, many clerical and police constable jobs require only 12th pass qualification.

Are state government jobs permanent?

Yes, most state government jobs are permanent and include pension benefits.

What is the salary of state PSC officers?

State PSC officers generally earn between ₹56,100 and ₹1,00,000 per month.
