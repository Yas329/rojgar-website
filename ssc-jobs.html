About SSC

SSC stands for Staff Selection Commission. It is one of the biggest government recruitment organizations in India. SSC conducts various exams every year for recruitment in ministries, departments, and government offices.

SSC provides jobs for 10th pass, 12th pass, diploma holders, and graduates.

SSC Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

  • 10th Pass for SSC MTS and SSC GD
  • 12th Pass for SSC CHSL and Stenographer
  • Graduate for SSC CGL and SSC CPO
  • Engineering Diploma/Degree for SSC JE

Age Limit

  • 18 to 27 years for most exams
  • Age relaxation for SC/ST/OBC categories

SSC Exam Syllabus

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is SSC?

SSC is Staff Selection Commission which recruits candidates for central government jobs.

Which is the best SSC exam?

SSC CGL is considered the best SSC exam because of salary and job profile.

Can 12th pass students apply for SSC?

Yes, students can apply for SSC CHSL and SSC Stenographer after 12th.

Is SSC difficult?

SSC exams are competitive but can be cracked with proper preparation.
