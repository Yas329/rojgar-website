About Police Jobs

Police jobs are among the most respected government jobs in India. Every year central and state governments release thousands of vacancies for Constable, Sub Inspector (SI), Inspector and other police posts.

Police jobs provide job security, good salary, government benefits and an opportunity to serve society.

Police Job Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • 10th Pass for some constable posts
  • 12th Pass for police constable recruitment
  • Graduation required for SI and officer posts

Physical Requirements

  • Running test mandatory
  • Height and chest measurement required
  • Medical fitness compulsory

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which is the best police job?

Sub Inspector and IPS officer posts are considered among the best police jobs.

Can 12th pass students apply for police jobs?

Yes, many police constable recruitments require only 12th pass qualification.

Is physical fitness compulsory for police jobs?

Yes, physical and medical fitness are mandatory for most police recruitments.

What is the salary of Police Constable?

The salary of a Police Constable is around ₹25,000 to ₹40,000 per month.
