About Judiciary Jobs

Judiciary jobs are among the most respected government careers in India. These jobs include Civil Judge, High Court Assistant, Supreme Court Clerk, Legal Officer and other law-related government positions.

Judiciary jobs provide excellent salary, social respect, government facilities and career stability.

Judiciary Job Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • LLB Degree required for judge and legal posts
  • Graduation required for assistant and clerk posts
  • Typing knowledge required for some court jobs
  • Computer knowledge preferred in many recruitments

Age Limit

  • 21 to 35 years for Judicial Services Exams
  • 18 to 30 years for clerk and assistant posts
  • Age relaxation available for reserved categories

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which is the best judiciary job?

Civil Judge and Judicial Magistrate are considered among the best judiciary jobs.

Is LLB compulsory for judiciary exams?

Yes, LLB degree is compulsory for judge and legal officer posts.

Can graduates apply for court clerk jobs?

Yes, many court clerk and assistant jobs require graduation qualification.

What is the salary of a Civil Judge?

Civil Judges generally earn between ₹77,000 and ₹1,36,000 per month.
