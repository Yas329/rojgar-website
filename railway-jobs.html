About Railway Jobs

Indian Railways is one of the largest government employers in India. Every year Railway Recruitment Boards (RRB) release thousands of vacancies for 10th pass, 12th pass, diploma holders and graduates.

Railway jobs are famous for good salary, job security, free travel benefits and pension facilities.

Railway Job Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • 10th Pass for Railway Group D
  • 12th Pass for some NTPC posts
  • Graduate for NTPC Graduate Posts and RPF SI
  • ITI or Diploma for ALP and Technician
  • Engineering Degree/Diploma for JE

Age Limit

  • 18 to 33 years for most railway exams
  • Relaxation available for reserved categories

Railway Exam Syllabus

  • Mathematics
  • General Intelligence & Reasoning
  • General Awareness
  • Current Affairs
  • Technical Subjects (for JE & ALP)
Daily mock tests and current affairs preparation are very important for railway exams.

Why Students Prefer Railway Jobs?

  • Central government job security
  • Good salary and allowances
  • Free or discounted railway travel
  • Pension and medical facilities
  • Promotion opportunities
  • Large number of vacancies every year

Railway Exam Preparation Tips

  • Practice previous year papers
  • Improve speed in mathematics
  • Focus on reasoning shortcuts
  • Study current affairs daily
  • Attempt online mock tests regularly
  • Prepare technical subjects properly for ALP & JE

Best Books for Railway Exams

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which is the best railway job?

RRB JE and Station Master are considered among the best railway jobs.

Can 10th pass students apply for railway jobs?

Yes, students can apply for Railway Group D and Apprentice posts.

Is railway exam difficult?

Railway exams are competitive but can be cleared with proper preparation.

What is the salary of RRB ALP?

The salary of Assistant Loco Pilot is around ₹35,000 to ₹50,000 per month.
