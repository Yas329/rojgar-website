About Government Teaching Jobs

Government teaching jobs are among the most respected and stable careers in India. Every year central and state governments recruit teachers for primary, secondary and higher secondary schools.

Teaching jobs provide job security, good salary, holidays, pension benefits and social respect.

Teaching Job Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • D.El.Ed required for Primary Teacher
  • B.Ed required for TGT and PGT
  • Graduation or Post Graduation required
  • CTET or State TET qualification mandatory for many jobs

Age Limit

  • 18 to 40 years for most teaching recruitments
  • Age relaxation available for reserved categories

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which is the best teaching job?

KVS and Assistant Professor jobs are considered among the best teaching jobs.

Is CTET compulsory for government teaching jobs?

Yes, CTET or State TET qualification is compulsory for many government teaching recruitments.

Can graduates apply for teaching jobs?

Yes, graduates with B.Ed qualification can apply for many teaching posts.

What is the salary of a government teacher?

Government teacher salary generally ranges from ₹35,000 to ₹90,000 per month.
