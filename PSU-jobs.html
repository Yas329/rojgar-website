About PSU Jobs

PSU stands for Public Sector Undertaking. These are government-owned companies that recruit engineers, graduates, diploma holders and technicians every year. PSU jobs are among the highest paying government jobs in India.

PSU jobs offer excellent salary packages, job security, accommodation, medical benefits and career growth.

PSU Recruitment Through GATE

Many PSUs recruit engineers through GATE scores. Candidates with good GATE rank can get direct interview calls from companies like ONGC, NTPC, BHEL, IOCL and GAIL.

Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Electronics and Computer Science branches have the highest PSU recruitment opportunities.

PSU Job Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • B.Tech / BE for Engineer posts
  • Diploma for JE and Technician posts
  • Graduation for management and administrative posts
  • ITI qualification for trade apprentice jobs

Age Limit

  • 18 to 30 years for most PSU recruitments
  • Age relaxation available for reserved categories

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which is the best PSU company?

ONGC, NTPC, IOCL and BHEL are considered among the best PSU companies.

Is GATE compulsory for PSU jobs?

Many PSU companies recruit through GATE, but some also conduct direct recruitment.

Can diploma holders apply for PSU jobs?

Yes, diploma holders can apply for JE and technician posts.

What is the salary of PSU engineers?

PSU engineers generally earn between ₹60,000 and ₹1,20,000 per month.
