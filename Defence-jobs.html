About Defence Jobs

Defence jobs in India are among the most respected government jobs. Students can join Indian Army, Indian Navy, Indian Air Force, Paramilitary Forces and Defence Research Organizations.

Defence jobs provide national service, discipline, adventure, excellent salary and government facilities.

Defence Job Eligibility

Educational Qualification

  • 10th Pass for some soldier and tradesman posts
  • 12th Pass for NDA, Navy SSR and Air Force
  • Graduation required for CDS, AFCAT and CAPF

Physical Requirements

  • Physical fitness is mandatory
  • Medical fitness required
  • Running, height and chest standards apply

Defence Exam Syllabus

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Which is the best defence job?

NDA and CDS officer posts are considered among the best defence jobs.

Can 12th pass students apply for defence jobs?

Yes, students can apply for NDA, Agniveer Army, Navy and Air Force after 12th.

Is physical fitness compulsory?

Yes, physical and medical fitness are mandatory for defence jobs.

What is the salary of Army Soldier GD?

The salary of Army Soldier GD is around ₹30,000 to ₹40,000 per month.
